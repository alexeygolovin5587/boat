Our Fleet

 

Cruise the lordly waters of Ireland’s River Shannon with Silver Line Cruisers

Hire a cruiser and set sail on one of the finest waterways in Europe with Silver Line Cruisers, as the unspoilt River Shannon meanders through beautiful Irish countryside, past historic towns offering the best in Irish hospitality. Silver Line, Ireland’s longest established family run Shannon cruising company, has been in the business for over 40 years.

With our exceptional location, right in the heart of Ireland on the River Shannon, and our modern, luxurious fleet of 2 to 12 berth charter cruisers, we will offer you the opportunity of a holiday experience that will stir the soul and capture the imagination of the most adventurous spirit.

Silver Line Cruisers offers a wide variety of breaks, from three nights or longer mini-breaks to weekly, 10 -14 day hire or extended vacations from any day of the week.

Captaining your own cruiser requires no licence in Ireland, with full training provided – See our Terms and Conditions for more details. Our friendly staff are only too happy to help answer any questions you might have. – Contact us today to start your Shannon adventure.
